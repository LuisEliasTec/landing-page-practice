¡Traditional macarons!

Macarons are quite a complicated dessert to make, they are actually easy to prepare but it is difficult to find the perfect point. The secret is with the necessary consistency of the dough and finding the necessary temperature and height in our oven. A few tips, a few practices and you are sure to get the perfect macarons.

¡A different touch!

Actually, there is not much of a secret. A macaron de law is a perfect mix of almond flour and meringue. But its preparation has some complexities so it is better to read the entire recipe before putting the dough up your sleeve.
