Carrot cake!

With whole wheat flour, carrots, raisins and walnuts, a small slice of this classic cake, it goes a long way to please your taste buds!.

¡Chocolate cake!

The base is a versatile cake, devilishly dark and chocolaty, which is achieved by the use of chocolate and cocoa in the dough. A moist and fluffy cake although, if we take it directly from the fridge, the texture becomes denser and firmer..

¡Three milk cake!

Condensed milk, whole milk, and whipped cream are the traditional “three milks” that are mixed and poured into the cake. This recipe transforms an ordinary cake into a rich, moist, and great-tasting dessert.
